What is important about Aaron Copland?

Aaron Copland is arguably the composer who established the sound of American symphonic music. He is certainly the most well-known. Not only did Copland's work set the stage for our national sound, he influenced a generation of students and helped to educate the public about music.

Who were some of Copland's students that turned out to be famous in the world of music?

  • Samuel Adler
  • Elmer Bernstein
  • Leonard Bernstein
